IACSD

IACSD Akurdi is an Authorized Training Centre of C-DAC ACTS Centre for Development of Advanced Computing (C-DAC) is the premier R&D organization of the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) for carrying out R&D in IT, Electronics and associated areas. Different areas of C-DAC, had originated at different times, many of which came out as a result of identification of opportunities. ✓ The setting up of C-DAC in 1988 itself was to built Supercomputers in context of denial of import of Supercomputers by USA. Since then C-DAC has been undertaking building of multiple generations of Supercomputer starting from PARAM with 1 GF in 1988. ✓ Almost at the same time, C-DAC started building Indian Language Computing Solutions with setting up of GIST group (Graphics and Intelligence based Script Technology); National Centre for Software Technology (NCST) set up in 1985 had also initiated work in Indian Language Computing around the same period. ✓ Electronic Research and Development Centre of India (ER&DCI) with various constituents starting as adjunct entities of various State Electronic Corporations, had been brought under the hold of Department of Electronics and Telecommunications (now MeitY) in around 1988.

OVERVIEW

Dr. D. Y. Patil Pratishthan is a charitable trust engaged in education since the last 25 years. The Pratishthan has excellent educational complexes in Mumbai, Pune and Kolhapur, imparting quality education from kindergarten to post graduation. More than 50,000 students are undergoing education in different disciplines backed by around 3,000 well qualified staff. Listed below are some disciplines of education that the Pratishthan imparts at Pune, Mumbai & Kolhapur Institute for Advanced Computing and Software Development (IACSD) It is the outcome of visionary mission of Dr. D. Y. Patil, Founder President of Dr. D. Y. Patil Pratishthan. The Institute has been set up to fulfill the following objectives: • To nurture and develop skilled manpower in the field of IT and Electronics. • To impart courses with practical-centric approach. • To provide quality and latest information in the field of technology. • The core strength of the institute lies in the dynamic management team, excellent infrastructure, experienced and qualified faculties who have been trained to deliver the latest and most advanced technological knowledge to the students. Industry experts visit our institute from time to time and deliver lectures. • The Institute is also involved in other activities such as software solutions, product development, network services etc, which supports the IT industry. In the future, there are plans to expand the areas of services offered by the Institute. • The Institute is located in a tranquil environment within walking distance to the Akurdi railway station. All the necessary amenities are provided to the students to enable them to work in the institute round the clock. The institute is open 24x7 and free wi-fi internet access is provided to students throughout the campus.
